From Firefighting to Full-Stack Development?

Being a firefighter and a computer programmer may seem worlds apart, but both roles require quick problem-solving, precision under pressure, and teamwork to be successful. Firefighters must assess dangerous situations and respond with decisive action, just as programmers must debug code or manage system failures swiftly and effectively. Both professions also rely heavily on protocols—firefighters follow safety procedures, while programmers follow coding standards and best practices. Adaptability is key in both jobs, as no two fires or code issues are exactly the same, and both must be prepared to learn and evolve with new technologies and challenges.
